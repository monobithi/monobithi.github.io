The Editorial Board

Aritra Bandyopadhyay


Aritra can be called a painter, sculptor, pianist and photographer. He is a passionate listener of Hindustani Classical music alongside being a published author of two Bengali fictions : 'Durga Pujoy Ashur Nidhon' (2017) and 'Lukochuri' (2018). He believes Satyajit Ray to be his teacher or ‘guru’ in all fields, and his motto in life is “Art for art’s sake”.


Education : Don Bosco School, Park Circus, Kolkata; 4th Year BS-MS, Indian Institute of Science Education & Research (IISER), Mohali.

Khushi Bhattacharya


Khushi is a painter and an excellent Bharatnatyam dancer. Eager to absorb innovations. She is a regular performer in college theaters and a dedicated follower of Satyajit Ray.


Education : Holy Child School, Jalpaiguri; 4th Year BS-MS, Indian Institute of Science Education & Research (IISER), Mohali.

Rajarshi Mukherjee


Rajarshi is the one who expresses what he feels through his sharp witted poetry. A photographer and a regular and extensive reader, Rajarshi is an ardent critic of the three masters of Bengali Cinema (Rittwik Ghatak, Mrinal Sen and Satyajit Ray).


Education : Don Bosco School, Park Circus, Kolkata; 4th Year, BTech(ETCE), Jadavpur University, Kolkata.

Aheli Majumdar


Aheli is a passionate dancer of Bharatnatyam and a sportsperson. An avid reader and an ardent follower of Netaji and Satyajit Ray.


Education : Calcutta Girls High School, 3rd Year BTECH(ECE), Institute of Engineering & Management (IEM), Kolkata.
