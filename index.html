Monobithi Vol 3.1 - Spring 2025

Published digitally on 14th April, 2025


The issue boasts a special feature on Jamini Roy, heartwarming poems by Spandan, Sukanta, Pranaya, Rajarshi, Vaishnavi, and Kushankur, engaging proses by Aditi, Aritra and Khushi, and a commemorative editorial on Saradindu.

The issue is adorned with lensational moments captured by Aratrik, Suchismita, Swapnanil, Sayan, Spandan, and Rajarshi and is also decked with magnificent artworks by Asmita, Khushi, Sreeja, Ananya, Purnendu and Souham.
