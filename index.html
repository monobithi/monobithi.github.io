MONOBITHI
The Meanderings of Imagination

Hello voyager,

Welcome aboard on the ship — your very own Monobithi !!! You must’ve had a hard journey out there in the cold, harsh world, but don’t you worry ! You’ve hit the right place ! Time flows differently here, my friend. No hurries, no worries, just take your time and explore the new world that you’ve entered. And don’t forget to enjoy yourself in the process, for here enjoyment is mandatory and it comes for free !!! Then buckle up, and prepare yourself for a ride on the rollercoaster of imagination !! And don’t get lost on your exploration (there’s an explorer’s guide in the menu to help you out). It’s a long way and we hope you have a wonderful experience as you let your mind flow, for Monobithi is nothing but ‘The Meanderings of Imagination’ !!!
